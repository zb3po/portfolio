A little about me..

I was born and raised in Stockton, Ca.. Where I enjoyed life for the most part. I worked a number of interesting jobs in my youth before venturing down different paths. Some of them included doing things like making smoothies. Printing t-shirts. Answering 1-800-ALLSTATE calls. Then I eventually signed up for a 6 year apprenticeship program to train as a carpenter. I helped build houses, schools, hospitals, Microsoft, Google. We built A LOT, so.... I learned ALOT! Not only, how to build anything, but also how to "make it work". I've learned to apply that to every aspect of my life now. I went on to learn how to grow my own food, and medicine, and a number of other DIY projects like building custom hydroponic and aquaponic garden setups. That opened doors for me to begin a path into the Hydroponics industry, where I was able to create my own positions and delve into research and devolpment and testing roles, and ultimately being asked to operate and oversee numerous cannabis cultivation facilities and operations. We did everything from seed breeding and cultivating to massive plant cloning and flowering facilities. These places are biginning to be extremely high-tech, and for the most part- I'd say 80-90% of the processes are now fully automated. Looking back at the construction background I came from, all of the blueprints are now digital. People are now walking around the job sites with tablets instead of real prints. Gardens are being automated on every level to support massive market demands. Trillions of dollars are going into tech and automation to support the current direction and demands of an increasingly online society.. So if we're going full-on 'Ready Player One', I'm here for it. Let's build!
