Why do I want to learn how to code?

I've been tinkering with everything I could get my hands on since I was a kid; began building things soon after. Recently, I've become intrigued by the recent explosive adoption rate of cryptocurrencies, and the passion behind the community working behind the scenes of the blockchain technology. Since becomeing exposed to my first cryptocurrency last year, it's quickly become my main area of interest. Learning how to operate mining software, and run validator nodes are just a few things that I have been involved in lately that have really inspired a burning desire to dig deeper into the code, and take my carpentry skills into the tech realm and see what I will be able to create as I learn and continue to be inspired!

  • SaaS Products
  • PoW Blockchain (proof of work)
  • PoS Blockchain (proof of stake)
  • Smart Contracts
